Scrambled Eggs

Eggs are one of my favorite fooods. Here is a recipe forr delicious rich scambled eggs.

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 1tbs butter
  • 2tbs cream

Method

  • Melt butter in a frying pan over a medium heat
  • Gently mix the eggs and cream in a bowl
  • Once butter has melted adđ cream and eggs
  • Using a spatula fold the eggs from the edge of the pan to the center every 20 secands(as if you are making an omelette)
  • When the eggs are still moist remove the heat(it will continue to cook on the plate until servel)
